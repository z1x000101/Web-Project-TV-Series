Factions & Alliances

The battle for New York City is fought in boardrooms and back alleys.

Danny Rand
The Immortal Iron Fist
Orphaned billionaire who survived a plane crash, trained in the mystical city of K'un-Lun for 15 years, and defeated a dragon to earn the power of the Iron Fist.
Colleen Wing
Daughter of the Dragon
A fierce martial arts instructor running the Chikara Dojo in NYC. She wields a custom white katana and brutally protects her city from gang warfare.
Ward Meachum
Rand Corp Executive
Danny's skeptical childhood friend who now runs Rand Enterprises. He struggles with substance abuse while secretly covering up his father's undead existence.
Joy Meachum
Corporate Shark
Co-CEO of Rand Enterprises. Initially ruthless in rejecting Danny's return, her loyalties are constantly tested between her brother, her presumed-dead father, and her childhood friend.
Davos
The Steel Serpent
Danny's brother-in-arms in K'un-Lun. Infuriated that Danny abandoned his post after winning the Iron Fist, Davos seeks to violently rip the power from him and execute his own bloody justice.
Madame Gao
Leader of The Hand
An ancient, deceptively frail woman leading the supernatural ninja syndicate known as The Hand. She controls massive narcotics operations and wields deadly telekinetic martial arts.
