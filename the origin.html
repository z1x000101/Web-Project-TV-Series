The Immortal Weapon

Forged in tragedy. Hardened in K'un-Lun. Tested in New York.

The Himalayan Crash

When ten-year-old Danny Rand was flying to China with his billionaire parents, a tragic crash left him orphaned and stranded deep within the frozen Himalayas. As the blizzard closed in, he was rescued by mysterious warrior monks and swept away through a magical portal into the legendary, hidden city of K'un-Lun.

Training in K'un-Lun

For fifteen arduous years, Danny endured brutal monastic martial arts conditioning under the strict tutelage of Lei Kung the Thunderer. He learned to weaponize his grief, transforming his trauma into pristine spiritual focus, slowly rising through the ranks to become K'un-Lun's greatest warrior.

Shou-Lao the Undying

To claim the ultimate prize, Danny Rand faced the immortal dragon, Shou-Lao the Undying. By plunging his fists into the dragon's molten heart, he survived the unbearable agony to channel the power of the Iron Fist, becoming capable of summoning pure, indestructible Chi.

The Return to New York

Barefoot and presumed dead for 15 years, Danny returns to New York City to reclaim his father's multibillion-dollar company, Rand Enterprises. But he soon realizes that the city is deeply infected by his ancient, sworn enemy: the ninja assassin cult known as The Hand.
