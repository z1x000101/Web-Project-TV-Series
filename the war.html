Campaign Timeline

The defense of New York and the legacy of K'un-Lun.

Season 1

The Return & The Hand

Danny Rand violently resurfaces in New York City after a 15-year disappearance in the mystical city of K'un-Lun. As he attempts to reclaim his billionaire inheritance from his childhood friends Ward and Joy Meachum, he discovers his company has been deeply infiltrated by The Hand, an ancient ninja cult. Guided by his Iron Fist powers and allying with martial artist Colleen Wing, Danny wages a brutal corporate and physical war against Madame Gao and the secretly resurrected Harold Meachum to reclaim his destiny.

Season 2

The Steel Serpent

With K'un-Lun mysteriously vanished and New York engulfed in gang warfare, Danny steps up as the downtown protector. However, his mission is shattered when his adoptive K'un-Lun brother, Davos, arrives in the city. Boiling with jealousy and radical ideology, Davos violently steals the power of the Iron Fist through an ancient ritual. Powerless and broken, Danny must retrain his mind and body under Colleen's guidance to stop the Steel Serpent from bathing the streets in self-righteous bloodshed.

The Defenders

The Grand Crossover

Alongside his solo efforts, Danny Rand acts as the critical mystical anchor in The Defenders. Uniting with Daredevil, Jessica Jones, and Luke Cage, the Immortal Iron Fist acts as the key to stopping The Hand from harvesting dragons buried beneath New York City to achieve true immortality.
